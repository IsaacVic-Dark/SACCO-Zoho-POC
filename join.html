Terms & Conditions

1. Membership

By joining Umoja Sacco, you agree to uphold the cooperative's bylaws and contribute regularly to your savings account. Membership is open to Kenyan citizens and residents aged 18 and above.

2. Contributions

A minimum monthly contribution of KSh 500 is required. Failure to contribute for three consecutive months may result in account suspension pending review.

3. Loans

Loan eligibility is based on your savings balance and creditworthiness as assessed by the SACCO. Loans are subject to approval by the credit committee.

4. Dividends

Dividends are declared annually at the AGM and are based on the SACCO's financial performance. Past dividend rates do not guarantee future returns.

5. Data Privacy

Your personal data is processed in accordance with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019. We will not share your data with third parties without your consent except where required by law.

6. Account Closure

You may withdraw from membership by giving 90 days written notice. All outstanding loans must be settled before membership is terminated.
